# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Fork Leads, BnB core staff/leadership, and external people/organization members booking meetings with the team.

## Product Purpose
A custom scheduling portal for the Bits&Bytes community that manages local availability, coordinate syncs, and provisions temporary Discord voice channels.

## Brand Personality
Tech-minimalist, sleek, developer-native.
Key words: High-performance, Restrained, Precise.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic bootstrap admin dashboards, heavy gradients, slow/bouncy animations, side-stripe borders, or text gradients.

## Design Principles
- **Familiarity & Speed**: Optimize for fast, standard productivity UI conventions (like Linear/Vercel).
- **Aesthetic Restraint**: Keep secondary elements quiet, using brand burgundy `#97192c` only for key actions and focus states.
- **Developer-Native Polish**: Monospace/terminal accents, crisp borders, micro-interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios meeting WCAG AA standard.
- Responsive mobile/tablet sizing.
- Strict support for `prefers-reduced-motion`.
